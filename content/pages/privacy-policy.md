**Effective Date:** 01/08/2026

Austin STEM Center (“we,” “our,” or “us”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect personal information when you visit our website, register for programs, donate, or otherwise interact with us.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

### Personal Information

- Name

- Email address

- Phone number

- Mailing address

- Child/student name, age, and grade level (for program registration)

- Emergency contact information

- Payment and billing information (processed securely by third-party providers)

### Non-Personal Information

- Browser type and device information

- Pages visited and time spent on our website

- General location data (city/state)

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

- Register students for classes, camps, and events

- Communicate program updates, schedules, and reminders

- Process payments and donations

- Respond to inquiries and support requests

- Improve our programs, website, and services

- Send newsletters or promotional information (you may opt out at any time)

- Meet legal, safety, and organizational requirements

---

## 3. Children’s Privacy

Austin STEM Center provides programs for children. We collect information about children **only with parental or guardian consent** and solely for educational, safety, and administrative purposes.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate consent, nor do we sell or share children’s data for marketing purposes.

---

## 4. Sharing of Information

We do **not** sell or rent personal information.

We may share information only with:

- Trusted service providers (e.g., registration platforms, payment processors, email services) necessary to operate our programs

- Legal or regulatory authorities when required by law

- Emergency responders when necessary to protect health and safety

All third parties are expected to handle data responsibly and securely.

---

## 5. Payment Processing

Payments and donations are processed through secure third-party providers. Austin STEM Center does **not** store full credit card information on our servers.

---

## 6. Cookies & Analytics

Our website may use cookies or similar technologies to:

- Improve website functionality

- Understand how visitors use our site

- Enhance user experience

You can disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features may not function properly.

---

## 7. Email Communications

If you provide your email address, we may send:

- Program-related communications

- Newsletters or announcements

- Fundraising or event information

You may unsubscribe at any time using the link provided in our emails.

---

## 8. Data Security

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and physical measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure.

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

---

## 9. Your Rights & Choices

You may:

- Request access to your personal information

- Request corrections or updates

- Request deletion of your information, subject to legal or operational requirements

- Opt out of marketing communications

To make a request, contact us using the information below.

---

## 10. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites.

---

## 11. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
