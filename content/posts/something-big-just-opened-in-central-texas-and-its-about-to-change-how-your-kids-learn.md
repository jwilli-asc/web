There are moments you don’t realize you’ve been waiting for… until they finally arrive.

This is one of those moments.

After years of dreaming, months of planning, and a team of people who are deeply passionate about what they do, we have brought something to life that we believe will change how kids experience learning, not just in Central Texas but across STEAM education as a whole.

Austin STEM Center is officially opening and summer camp registration is now LIVE.

And before you think, “Oh, just another summer camp…” I would encourage you to pause for a second. Because this isn’t that.

This is the kind of place where something shifts for your child. Where learning stops feeling like something they *have* to do and starts feeling like something they* want* to do. Where curiosity turns into confidence, and ideas turn into something they can actually hold in their hands.

You know that moment when your child gets really into something, when they stop asking for help and start saying, “Wait, I want to try it myself”? That moment where you can almost see their confidence building in real time?

That’s what we built this place for.

At Austin STEM Center, rather than starting with curriculum we start with the child. Where they are, what they’re naturally drawn to, how they think, how they learn. Because when you meet a child there, instead of forcing them into a box, something powerful happens. They engage. They explore. They start to believe, “Maybe I *am* good at this.”

And when that belief clicks, everything changes.

Our instructors show up knowing they are shaping how kids see themselves. Not just what they can learn, but who they believe they can become. Because when a child builds something real—tests it, breaks it, fixes it, and sees it work—their brain locks onto that experience in a way no worksheet ever could. That’s how kids stop doubting themselves and start trusting what they’re capable of.

We’ve created a space designed to make that happen. A space where your child walks into an electronics lab, a woodshop, a metal shop, a makerspace and instead of just looking around, they’re actually *using* it. They’re learning how to operate real machines. 3D printers, CO₂ and fiber lasers, tools they didn’t even know they could touch yet. This is real-world, hands-on, “I can’t believe I just made that” kind of learning.

And our summer camp lineup? It’s built to blow their minds.

We’re taking the things kids already love and turning them into something tangible, something challenging, something that builds real skill and confidence.

If your kid loves Minecraft, imagine them stepping out of the screen and into a space where they’re designing and building structures they can actually stand inside. Testing them, improving them, bringing their imagination into the real world.

If your kid loves speed, competition, figuring out how things work, imagine them building and rebuilding racecars, learning how performance is actually created through precision, tuning, and smart design. Understanding on a foundational level that winning isn’t luck, it’s built.

If your kid is creative, curious, loves music or tinkering, imagine them soldering circuits, creating sound from scratch, building something that didn’t exist before they touched it and walking away thinking, “Wait… I made that?”

And for the teens who have bigger ideas, the ones who are constantly thinking, constantly creating, constantly asking “what if?” we’ve created a space for them too. A place where they can finally build the things they’ve been dreaming about with the guidance, tools, and support to actually bring it to life.

We’ve built entry points for every level, every age, every kind of learner—from 6 to 18—because every child deserves the chance to feel capable, to feel confident, to feel like they belong in spaces like this.

But here’s the part you need to know: Spots are limited.

Not because we want them to be but because they have to be.

We are intentionally keeping class sizes smaller so every child gets the attention, support, and space they need to actually have this experience. This only works if we do it right. Which means the parents who move first are the ones who secure these spots. And if you’re anything like most parents, you’ve probably had that moment before where you saw something, thought, “That would be perfect,” waited a little too long… and then it was gone.

We don’t want that to be this. Because if you’re feeling something right now, if you’re reading this and thinking, “This is exactly what my child needs” that feeling matters.

Imagine it’s the end of summer. Your child isn’t just saying, “Camp was fun.” They’re showing you what they built. Explaining how it works. Talking about what they want to create next. Carrying themselves a little differently. Thinking a little bigger. Believing in themselves in a way you can actually see. And you’re realizing this wasn’t just something to fill their time. It changed something in them. That’s what this is about.

If you’ve been looking for something more for your child, more engaging, more meaningful, more impactful, this is it.

Secure your spot now before sessions fill. Because this summer is about showing them what they’re capable of.
